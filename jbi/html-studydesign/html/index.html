ETHOS and KRONOS are part of the ATHENA Phase III clinical trial program for TRIXEO

ETHOS

Randomised, double-blind, multicenter, parallel-group, 52-week trial evaluated the efficacy and safety of TRIXEO in symptomatic patients with moderate to very severe COPD and a history of moderate or severe exacerbation(s) in the previous year.1,2

KRONOS

Randomised, double-blind, multicenter, parallel-group, 24-week trial evaluated the efficacy and safety of TRIXEO in symptomatic patients with moderate to very severe COPD with no requirement for a moderate or severe exacerbation in the previous year.1,3

Efficacy
LUNG FUNCTION1

TRIXEO AEROSPHERE MDI 320/18/9.6 mcg demonstrated an increase in on-treatment FEV1 AUC0-4 and trough FEV1 at Week 24 relative to ICS/LABA MDI 320/9.6 mcg and LAMA/LABA MDI 18/9.6 mcg (sub-study primary endpoints).*

There were consistent improvements in lung function in patient subgroups classified by age, sex, degree of airflow limitation (moderate, severe, and very severe), and previous ICS use.

SECONDARY ENDPOINT2: RESCUE MEDICATION USE1

Patients treated with TRIXEO AEROSPHERE MDI 320/18/9.6 mcg on average used less daily rescue medication over 24 weeks compared to patients treated with LAMA/LABA MDI 18/9.6 mcg and ICS/LABA MDI 320/9.6 mcg.

*A subset of patients were included in a spirometric sub-study with primary endpoints of FEV1 AUC0-4 at Week 24 (mL) and change from baseline in morning pre-dose trough FEV1 at Week 24 (mL).
