Lung cancer is the most common cancer and the leading cause of cancer-related deaths worldwide

  • Lung cancer is responsible for the largest number of cancer deaths (more than 1.7 million/year) worldwide3
  • The overall 5-year survival rate for lung cancer is 18% and varies by stage2

ADDRESS EMERGING POPULATIONS

  • Even with transformational therapies, most patients develop primary or acquired resistance
  • Define new resistance spectrum in the clinic
  • Establish new treatments to address innate and acquired resistance
  • Extend survival of patients by delaying acquired resistance
