Welcome to the IMPACT Academy

An initiative developed for Healthcare Professionals. The goal of the IMPACT academy is to provide educational resources and events which support the transformation of care provision, prevent the onset of and improve Patient outcomes in the cardiovascular, renal and metabolic setting.
